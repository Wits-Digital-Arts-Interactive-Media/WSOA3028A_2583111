Week Six

  • Takealot.com

    Takealot is an online retailer from South Africa which offers a wide variety of products. It provides users with a convenient platform to shop online with features like product reviews, multiple payment options, and nationwide delivery.

    Pros

    Takealot offers a wide range of products across various categories, catering to diverse consumer needs, and allows users to easily browse these products through its search bar or its multitude of filters.

    Cons

    The homepage can appear cluttered with promotions, advertisements, and deals, potentially overwhelming users

  • Ebay.com

    eBay is a global online marketplace where users can buy and sell a vast array of goods.

    Pros

    Ebay has a vast international user base, providing access to a wide range of products from around the world.

    Cons

    While the eBay app offers extensive functionality, its interface can be overwhelming for new users, potentially hindering the onboarding process.
